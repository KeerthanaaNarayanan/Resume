Smart Enterprise

  1. Mastery

    Connecting everyday objects like smart-phones, electrical devices, sensors and actuators to the Internet where the devices are intelligently linked together enabling new forms of communication between things and people, and between things themselves.

  2. Notifications

    Notifications are send to the controller of the building for any defectiveness of the devices present in the building. Intruder alerts are also included here.

  3. Parking

    Parking map is available to the controller to have an eye on the parking.

  4. Air Quality Check

    The Quality of the air is shared with the controller spontaneously.

  5. Manage Waste

    The proper management of waste in the building is shown in the link.

  6. Water Quality Check

    The Quality of the water in the building is always shared to the controller.

Various devices can be connected here. We are connecting two to three devices as samples.
